## 4. Caveats the wiring must carry (from verifier + critic)

1. **Clock-choice dependence (methodological risk):** Gielen & Menéndez-Pidal (arXiv:2005.05357, CQG 37, 205018; + 2205.15387) show singularity resolution/bounce physics can DEPEND on which relational clock is quantized. Adopting τ ∝ ∫dS as THE aeon clock must state that flash-crossing physics may be clock-dependent — check before any quantitative seam claim.
2. **The dS-tail attack:** Dyson–Kleban–Susskind (hep-th/0208013): Poincaré recurrences → no persistent arrow in an eternal causal patch. LMU's standing answer: the tail is CUT deterministically at ~10¹⁰⁰ yr ≪ e^(10¹²²) recurrence — must be stated *explicitly* wherever "τ crawls but never stops" is claimed.
3. **Rival monotone:** Brown–Susskind complexity (arXiv:1701.01107) — the canonical alternative "what keeps running when entropy saturates". Belongs in the adjacent-owner menu.
4. **Kiefer–Zeh is the opposite family** (arrow reversal at turning point) — contrast citation only.
5. **Barontini's own limits:** τ undefined where entropy does not flow; minisuperspace analogue; V≃0 regime.
6. **Coarse-grained only (Page-curve trap; added by the gap-fill pass):** under unitary evaporation the *fine-grained* radiation entropy is **non-monotonic** — it peaks at the Page time (~54% of the BH lifetime; Page arXiv:1301.4995) and then *decreases*. A τ_aeon built on fine-grained S_rad would run BACKWARD after the Page time. The wiring must integrate the **coarse-grained/thermodynamic** entropy (Zurek's ledger) — state this in the definition, not in a footnote.
7. **Near-seam clock reliability (GFT echo of caveat 1):** Marchetti–Oriti arXiv:2010.09700 — near a bounce the clock's own quantum fluctuations can invalidate relational evolution; the τ_aeon surge at the flash sits exactly in that regime.
