## What was built

A single reproducible chain from an old aeon's cold, clumpy end to a hot, smooth start with the real CMB:

1. **Energy half** — promote the conformal factor Ω = e^ω to a dynamical scalar (an α-attractor inflaton). It reheats to a GUT-scale hot start: V¹ᐟ⁴ ≈ 10¹⁶ GeV, T_reheat ≈ 3.5×10¹⁵ GeV, H_inf/M_p ≈ 10⁻⁵. Energy delivered by the inflationary free lunch (E=ρV at no net cost).
2. **Smooth half** — the old aeon's Weyl/inhomogeneity residual is diluted by inflation (e⁻²ᴺ per point) and, spread across the ~e³ᴺ Hubble patches, averages to e⁻⁵ᴺ. At N≈57 the per-point residual is ~10⁻⁴⁹ (44 orders below the 10⁻⁵ that seeds structure); spread across the aeon it reaches ~10⁻¹²² = the de Sitter / cosmological-constant floor. Not zero — below the level that has physical meaning.
3. **N-convergence** — three independent requirements land on the same N: CMB tilt (n_s=0.965 → N=57), residual dilution to the de Sitter floor (10⁻¹²² → N=56), horizon/flatness (N~55–60). One amount of inflation does all three jobs.
4. **CMB fit** — the same ω-inflaton, as an α-attractor with α≈2.44 at N≈57, reproduces Planck: n_s=0.9649, r=0.009, A_s=2.1×10⁻⁹ (δT/T = √A_s = 4.6×10⁻⁵). The CMB is written **fresh** by the new aeon's inflaton quantum modes, decoupled from the erased (no-hair) old aeon — residual and CMB differ by ~44 orders, consistent not contradictory.
